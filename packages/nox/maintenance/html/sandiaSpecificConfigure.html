NOX Compiling Guidelines

Supported platforms

  • ASCI Red (Intel Tflops)
  • ASCI Blue-mountain (SGI)
  • ASCI White (IBM w/KCC compiler)
  • CPlant (Alpha chips with Red-Hat Linx)
  • Linux (primary development platform)
  • Solaris

General Comments

  • The testing on specific platforms is out of date except for ASCI Red, Cplant, and Solaris (RPP 6/25/02).

ASCI Red (TFLOP)


    With Epetra/Trilinos tested 6/25/02
  • Cross-compiling for janus.sandia.gov on solaris: 
    
% ../configure CXXCPP="iCC -E" CXX=iCC CPPFLAGS="-DTFLOP
-I/Net/local/share/intel/tflop/R4.2.1/tflops/include/CC/RW" F77="if77
-I/Net/local/share/intel/tflop/R4.2.1/tflops/include/CC/RW"
LDFLAGS="-L/Net/local/share/intel/tflop/R4.2.1/tflops/lib/RW
-L/Net/pecos/rf_crew/Moved_to_806_netapp/people/rppawlo/arch/tflop/lib
-L/Net/pecos/home1/rppawlo/Trilinos/external/y12m/lib/TFLOP.MPI"
--with-mpilib=-lmpi
--with-y12m-libdir="-L/Net/pecos/home1/rppawlo/Trilinos/external/y12m/lib/TFLOP.MPI"
--enable-epetra
--disable-examples --host=i686

% make
